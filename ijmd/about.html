Our Mission

IJMD exists to accelerate the global conversation at the intersection of intelligent systems and data science. We believe peer-reviewed knowledge should be freely accessible to every researcher, practitioner, and student — regardless of institutional affiliation or geography.

We publish across six specialized series, each with a dedicated editorial board of domain experts, ensuring that every paper receives the most informed and contextually appropriate review possible. Our double-blind process, average 18-day first decision, and commitment to constructive feedback set a standard we hold ourselves to with every submission.

Operationally, IJMD is powered by VérgelAI — whose infrastructure handles submission management, review assignment, production, and distribution at global scale while preserving the editorial independence that gives our publications their authority.

History
2014
Founded. IJMD launches Series A (Machine Learning) and Series B (Data Science) with 12 inaugural papers.
2016
Series C launched. Robotics & Autonomous Systems added; IJMD accepted into Scopus.
2017
Series D launched. Edge Computing & Embedded Intelligence; first IJMD Annual Symposium held in Singapore.
2019
Series E launched. Human-Machine Interaction & Ethics. 5,000th paper published. SCI-Expanded indexing achieved.
2020
Full Open Access. All articles made freely available under CC BY 4.0. Partnership with VérgelAI for platform infrastructure.
2021
Series F launched. Foundation Models & Generative AI — the first dedicated peer-reviewed venue for large-scale pre-trained models.
2024
10th Anniversary. 10,000+ articles, 62 countries, Impact Factor 2.4. Series F accepted into SCI-Expanded.
2026
Today. 12,400+ articles. IF 2.8. 3.2M annual downloads. 186 board members. ijmd.org fully redesigned on VérgelAI platform.
