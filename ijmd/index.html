Scalable Attention Mechanisms for Real-Time Sensor Fusion in Autonomous Industrial Robots

We present SARTI, a transformer-based architecture that fuses heterogeneous sensor streams — LiDAR, tactile, and infrared — at sub-10ms latencies without sacrificing context window depth. Validated across seven industrial environments, achieving 97.3% object classification accuracy under heavy occlusion — a 14-point improvement over all existing baselines. Our ablation studies reveal that cross-modal positional encoding is the dominant contributor to performance gains.

Federated Learning Under Non-IID Conditions: Convergence Analysis with Differential Privacy Guarantees

We provide the first tight convergence bounds for federated optimization with heterogeneous data distributions under (ε,δ)-differential privacy. Our analysis reveals a fundamental tension between privacy budget and convergence rate that prior work had not quantified, enabling practitioners to make principled trade-offs. Experiments across four real-world healthcare and finance datasets confirm theoretical predictions within a 2% margin.

A Unified Framework for Sim-to-Real Transfer in Contact-Rich Manipulation Tasks

We address the sim-to-real gap for dexterous manipulation by combining domain randomization, tactile simulation, and online adaptation. Our framework transfers policies trained entirely in simulation to seven real-world manipulation tasks with zero additional real-world data collection. Success rates of 82–94% across tasks represent state-of-the-art for contact-rich manipulation without real-world training data.

Causal Discovery in High-Dimensional Time-Series Data from Manufacturing Lines

We apply constraint-based and score-based causal discovery methods to 18-month sensor logs from three automotive assembly lines, identifying 94 previously unknown causal dependencies that explain 78% of quality-control failures. Our open-source toolkit reduces expert analysis time from weeks to under four hours, with robustness guarantees under measurement noise levels typical of industrial IoT deployments.
